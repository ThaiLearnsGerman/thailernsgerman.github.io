Teacher rule
Don’t learn everything at once. Small daily wins = fluent later.

Start Learning in 10 Steps

Follow this order. Each step is small, practical, and “real life”.

1) Sounds
Ä / Ö / Ü / ß + pronunciation
2) Greetings
Hello, goodbye, polite basics
3) Sentence pattern
S + V + O
4) Articles
der / die / das (simple rules)
5) 10 words/day
Daily vocabulary system
6) Verbs
sein / haben / gehen / machen
7) Questions
W-questions + yes/no
8) Mini dialogues
Ordering, travel, meeting
9) Listening
Short daily audio practice
10) 4-week plan
Routine + checkpoints
